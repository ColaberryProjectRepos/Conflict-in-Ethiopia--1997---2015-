Problem Statement 1

Ethnic conflicts in Ethiopia are a major barrier to development. These conflicts followed by public resentment have resulted in the losse of human life and resources including the destruction of infrastructures and forced internal migration of millions. From 1997 to 2015 there have been 21,761 that is an average of 95 death per month as a result of conflict between Ethnic based rebel forces and the Government as reported by BBC and others.
